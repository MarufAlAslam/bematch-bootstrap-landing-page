Eli and Jacob

When you give, you support
patients like Eli and Jacob.

Both brothers had health challenges from the start. After years of unexplained illnesses, their parents learned they had IPEX syndrome, a rare genetic disorder. Without a bone marrow transplant, they likely wouldn’t live past 30. The situation had become dire.

Eli and Jacob with their donor, Avi

But then, everything changed.

Through the Be The Match Registry®, they found a life-saving donor. In November 2020, both received a transplant that would put them on the path to recovery. “It has been life changing for them,” their mom says. With hope on the horizon, Eli and Jacob are back at school, learning to read, and finally enjoying their childhood.
